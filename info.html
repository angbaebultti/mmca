MMCA SHOP

| 1F lobby

Showcasing the identity of MMCA Shop Seoul through curated merchandise inspired by art, exhibitions, and education.

MMCA art book shop

| 1F lobby

MMCA art book shop offers not only the publications of MMCA, including various exhibition catalogues but also a wide selection of art books.

Osulloc

| 1F lobby

Tea House Osulloc is a complex tea culture space located in MMCA Seoul, boasting a panoramic view of the museum's outer yard and beautiful Samcheong-dong.

TERAROSA

| 1F lobby

TERAROSA COFFEE offers guests cozy and relaxed ambience. Besides house beverages and bakeries, the store serves espresso and drip coffee from different countries.

DOORE

DOORE is a Korean restaurant of Korean tasty and tradition, offering from snacks and one-dish meals to full course meals.
